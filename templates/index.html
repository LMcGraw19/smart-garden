About The Lions' Gate

The Lions' Gate is located at the Edinburgh Napier Univercity's Mercheston campus, adjacent to the univercity Library. It is a inclusive sustainability gardens project that provides a garden, allotment, an outdoor laboratory and more, and aims at creating the right conditions for people to empower themselves.
